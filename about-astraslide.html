All About Astraslide?

Astraslide is a leading provider of premium swimming pool equipment, offering everything from pumps and filters to heaters and cleaning systems. We are committed to supplying only the best products, ensuring your pool stays in top condition all year round.

In addition to equipment sales, Astroslide excels in custom pool installation and building services. Our experienced team turns your vision into reality, crafting stunning pools that enhance your outdoor space.

We specialise in the supply and installation of in-ground pre-moulded fibreglass swimming pools, Spa pools (Hot tub or Jacuzzi), swimming pool equipment and general swimming pool supplies including chemicals in Nigeria.

Following the exposure of our team in Europe and America we have found that fibreglass in-ground swimming pool design is the future of swimming pool construction due to its numerous advantages over the traditional concrete pool and other types of swimming pool construction in Nigeria.
