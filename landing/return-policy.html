Return Policy

At Astraslide, we are committed to ensuring your satisfaction with our products and services. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we’re here to help. Please review our return policy below:


Eligibility for Returns
  • Timeframe: Returns must be initiated within 30 days of receiving your item(s).
  • Condition: Items must be unused, in their original condition, and in the original packaging, including all accessories and documentation.
  • Proof of Purchase: A valid proof of purchase, such as a receipt or order confirmation, is required for all returns.
  • Non-Returnable Items: Customized or special-order items, as well as items marked as “final sale,” are not eligible for return unless they arrive damaged or defective.

Return Process
  • Contact Us: Email our customer support team at info@astraslide.co to request a return authorization.
  • Provide Details: Include your order number, a description of the issue, and photos if the item is damaged or defective.
  • Return Authorization: Once approved, you will receive a return authorization number and detailed instructions on how to proceed.
  • Shipping: Ship the item(s) to the address provided. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to an error on our part or a defective product.

Refunds
  • Processing Time: Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days of receiving the returned item(s).
  • Method of Refund: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. If you paid by credit or debit card, the timing of the refund depends on your card issuer’s policies.
  • Deductions: If applicable, original shipping charges and restocking fees may be deducted from your refund.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange an item, please follow the return process to receive a refund and place a new order for the desired item.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive an item that is damaged or defective, please notify us within 7 days of receipt. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you.
